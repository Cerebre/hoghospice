Orphanage Visiting Rules

In order to ensure the safety and well-being of the children, we ask that all visitors please adhere to the following rules during their visit:

  • Respect the privacy and personal space of the children and staff
  • Follow all instructions and guidelines provided by the staff.
  • Do not give gifts or money directly to the children without permission from the staff.
  • Do not take photographs or videos of the children without permission from the staff.
  • Do not make promises or commitments to the children without permission from the staff.
  • Do not disclose personal information about the children or staff to anyone without permission from the staff.
  • Do not bring any food or drinks into the orphanage without permission from the staff.
  • Do not bring any pets or animals into the orphanage.
  • Do not smoke or use any illegal substances while on the orphanage grounds.
  • Do not engage in any behavior that could be perceived as inappropriate or harmful to the children or staff.

By following these rules, we can ensure that the children in our care are safe and comfortable during your visit. Thank you for your cooperation!
