Our benefits

Why volunteer here?

Make a difference

Volunteering here can give you the opportunity to help our children in a meaningful way, in various ways such as providing emotional support, or participating in recreational activities

Giving back

Volunteering at our orphanage is a great way to give back to your community in your own little way and make a positive impact on your society, and the world at large

Gain new skills and experiences

Volunteering at this orphanage can provide you with valuable experiences that can help you to develop new skills and build your resume which in turn helps you land a paying role

Make new connections

Our current chain of volunteers comprise of people from different spheres, professions and walks of life who share similar interests and opinions in providing value.

Culture diversification

Our hospice consists of people from different tribes, and volunteering here can provide you with the opportunity to learn about a different culture and way of life

Interested in joining the team in any specific capacity?

We’ll love to have you as a part of the team - fill the form and we’ll be in touch to schedule a meeting to discuss this. Thank you for indicating interest to take out time to be a blessing to my children.
