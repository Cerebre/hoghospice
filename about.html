our story

Providing a home and a supportive environment for children who have been orphaned or abandoned

01.
20+ years of service

For over 20 years, our facility has provided a safe and loving home for children who have lost their parents or are unable to live with their families

02.
15,000+ orphaned children saved

Over the years, our organization has helped to save the lives of 15,000 orphaned children that have come from all walks of life, but thanks to our dedicated team and generous donors, they have been given the love, support, and opportunities they need to thrive.

03.
$10k+ total charities raised

We couldn't have done it without the generosity of our employees, donors, and community. Thank you for helping us make a difference in the lives of these deserving children.

Our Vision

Hearts of Gold aspirations for the future are simply to continue to deliver quality service to our special children. Providing expert palliative care in a serene, relaxing environment and improving our services from time to time, sharing and assisting medical professionals who are contributing to the ever increasing number of children with special needs in our care.

To train and develop our staff, especially in nursing, social care and counseling. We recognize the demand for children’s hospices and we will be looking at innovative ways to develop and expand our services to react and adapt to the ever growing needs of children with terminal and other life-threatening illnesses throughout our country, Nigeria.

In addition to the excellent facilities we already have, we need your support to provide the much needed learning and play resources to help in the delivery of qualitative services required for the benefit of our children.

Hearts of Gold services are provided free of charge. So we are entirely dependent on the generosity of individuals, organizations and corporate bodies for revenue and capital projects.

Every child deserve respect, unconditional love and care, whether able or disabled, I believe that every child requires a safe home where love and attention is given. Hearts of Gold Children Hospice is the first and only children’s hospice and respite care facility in Nigeria.
